Over the Christmas break

Directory

During the Christmas holiday, I saw First Act of Wicked, a musical film, twice. One is in the theatre, while the other is at a friend's house. That December, I listened to their soundtrack/music as Christmas songs. When I saw it at the theatre, I thoroughly enjoyed it. In some ways, it was a happy moment. The plot of Wicked is that Elphaba, a misunderstood young woman because of her green skin, and Galinda, a popular girl, become friends at Shiz University in Oz. After meeting the Wonderful Wizard of Oz, their friendship comes to a crossroads. Although it is a two-hour film, I would recommend it to anyone who is interested in it.
